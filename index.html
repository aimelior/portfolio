About Aimee LaFon

Aimee LaFon is a freelance writer, editor, and content manager adept in all stages of content creation. With a curious mindset and project-based mentality, Aimee has built a wide range of skills including web development, on and off-page SEO, proofreading, editing, and content management.

Aimee's ultimate goal in building these interdisciplinary skills is to be able to use her expertise in various niches to support and fortify small businesses, writers, and creatives as they attempt to make their dreams become a reality.

Some of the niches in which Aimee has significant experience (and an abundance of passion for) include:

  • Homesteading and Permaculture
  • Fiber Arts
  • Arts and Crafts
  • 3D Printing
  • Poetry and Literature
  • History and Pop History
  • Sustainability
  • Foraging and Bushcrafting
  • Outdoors, Camping, and Hiking
  • Fashion and Fashion Design
